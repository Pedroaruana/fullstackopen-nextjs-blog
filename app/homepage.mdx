# Blog app

Welcome to the **blog app** built as part of the
[Full Stack Open: Next.js](https://courses.mooc.fi/org/uh-cs/courses/full-stack-open-nextjs)
course.

## What you can do here

- Browse **blogs** added by users
- Register an account and **log in**
- Add your own blogs
- Manage your personal **reading list** on the *My page*
- Generate an **API token** to access your data programmatically

---

## Tech stack

- **Next.js 15** with the App Router and React 19
- **NextAuth.js** for authentication
- **Drizzle ORM** with **Postgres**
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling

Have fun!
